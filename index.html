S-through-Z Game

Inventors: Michael Walsberger & Stefan Starke

Year of Origin: 2024

Category: Language Game

The S-through-Z Game is a language-based social game invented in 2024 by German authors Michael Walsberger and Stefan Starke. It is based on a simple but brilliant principle: systematically replacing every "S" in spoken or written words with a "Z".
